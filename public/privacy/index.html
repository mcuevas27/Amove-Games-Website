Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

A-Move Games ("we", "us") operates a self-hosted social media management tool that connects to our Facebook and Instagram accounts to schedule and publish content on our behalf.

Information We Access

  • Facebook Page and Instagram account profile information
  • Ability to create, publish, and manage posts on our connected accounts

How We Use This Information

This tool is used exclusively by A-Move Games for internal social media management. We use it to schedule and publish content to our own social media accounts.

Data Sharing

We do not collect, store, or share any third-party user data. This application is used solely for managing our own social media presence.

Data Storage

Account connection tokens are stored securely on our private, self-hosted server. No user data is transmitted to external parties.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at smm@amovegames.com
